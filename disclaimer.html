Can I Drive?

Offline Usage

This webapp can be saved for offline use, to do so follow these steps:

  1. Go to the settings button
  2. Click 'Add to Home Screen'

Disclaimer

Can I Drive aims to provide an accurate blood alcohol estimate, however without actual blood testing true blood alcohol levels cannot be determined. As such this service should be used as a rough guide and by using the service you acknowledge the risks involved, by using the service you forfeit rights to bring a suit against Can I Drive and associated personnel and developers.

Get Involved
